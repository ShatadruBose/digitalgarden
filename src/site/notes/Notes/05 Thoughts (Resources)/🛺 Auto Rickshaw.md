# 🛺 Auto Rickshaw
## An Ultra-Detailed Game Design Concept

**Genre:** Open World Driving Simulation • Business Management • RPG • Life Simulation • City Exploration • Multiplayer (Optional)

---

# Abstract

**Auto Rickshaw** is a simulation game where the player begins as a new auto-rickshaw driver with a single rented vehicle and gradually builds a transportation empire. Unlike racing games, the objective is not speed but mastering the everyday life of an auto driver—finding passengers, navigating traffic, earning trust, maintaining the vehicle, expanding a fleet, and becoming an integral part of the city's transport network.

The game celebrates the culture, economy, humor, and challenges surrounding one of the world's most iconic three-wheeled vehicles.
