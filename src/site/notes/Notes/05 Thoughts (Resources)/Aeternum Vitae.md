## 🕊️ The Divine Life Arrow — **Aeternum Vitae**

> _“Where existence fractures, this arrow restores.  
> Where time collapses, this arrow rebuilds.  
> Where death claims dominion, this arrow denies it.”_

---

### 🌟 Origin

Forged in the eternal fires beyond mortal planes, **Aeternum Vitae** was crafted from:

- The **First Light of Creation**
    
- The Breath of the **Phoenix Eternal**
    
- A fragment of the **Tree of Infinite Dawn**
    
- A drop of primordial life-essence from before decay existed
    

This arrow does not merely heal — it **redefines biological and metaphysical law**.

---

## ⚔️ Powers of Aeternum Vitae

### 🩸 1. Absolute Healing

- Instantly repairs all cellular, spiritual, and dimensional damage.
    
- Removes curses, poisons, hexes, plagues, corruption, and soul fractures.
    
- Restores lost limbs, organs, memories, and even fragmented consciousness.
    

---

### ♻️ 2. Infinite Regeneration

- Grants adaptive regeneration beyond physical limitation.
    
- Healing accelerates exponentially with damage severity.
    
- Immune to decay, disease, aging, entropy, and timeline corruption.
    

---

### 🌌 3. Immortality Blessing

- Grants **True Immortality**:
    
    - No aging
        
    - No death by physical harm
        
    - No soul destruction
        
    - No conceptual erasure
        
- Existence becomes anchored beyond time and fate.
    
- Even if body and soul are destroyed simultaneously, the arrow reconstructs both.
    

---

### 🕯️ 4. Resurrection Dominion

- Revives the recently deceased instantly.
    
- Can restore those dead for centuries — even millennia.
    
- Rebuilds body, soul, aura, and destiny threads.
    
- Memory and personality are fully restored.
    

---

### ⚖️ 5. Supremacy Over Death

Even if confronted by the god of death such as:

- Hades
    
- Thanatos
    
- Yama
    

The arrow overrides their authority.  
It does not _challenge_ death — it **negates its jurisdiction**.

Death cannot claim what Aeternum Vitae has marked.

---

## 🏹 Appearance

- Shaft: Pure white-gold infused with living light
    
- Tip: Crystal prism radiating green and golden aura
    
- Feathers: Phoenix-plume wings that glow brighter near mortal injury
    
- When drawn, time slows; when released, silence falls

- A pillar of emerald-gold light descends.
    
- Heartbeat echoes like thunder.
    
- Wounds seal in reverse motion.
    
- If reviving the dead:
    
    - Air rushes into lungs.
        
    - Eyes glow briefly.
        
    - Soul re-enters body in visible light form.

## ⚠️ Limitation

There is only **one condition**:

The arrow cannot be used for selfish domination or conquest.  
If intent is corrupted, the arrow dissolves into pure light and vanishes for a century.

Life cannot be weaponized.

---

## 🛡️ Final Truth

Aeternum Vitae is not a tool of war.  
It is the embodiment of **existence refusing extinction**.
