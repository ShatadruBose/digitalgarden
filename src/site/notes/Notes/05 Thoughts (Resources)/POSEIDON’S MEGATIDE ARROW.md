# 🌊 **POSEIDON’S MEGATIDE ARROW**

---

## 🔱 Name Engraving

Along the shaft, carved in glowing ancient Greek script:

**“ΠΟΣΕΙΔΩΝ” (POSEIDON)**  
The letters shimmer in deep ocean-blue light, like bioluminescent algae in the abyss.

---

## 🏹 Physical Design

### Shaft

- Material: Condensed Abyssal Coral fused with deep-sea obsidian.
    
- Color: Midnight blue with swirling internal currents visible inside the shaft.
    
- Texture: Smooth like polished driftwood, but constantly dripping spectral water that evaporates into mist.
    

### Arrowhead

- Shape: Three-pronged like a miniature trident (inspired by the weapon of Poseidon).
    
- Composition: Pressurized ocean-core crystal.
    
- Constantly surrounded by rotating rings of liquid water suspended in zero gravity.
    

### Fletching

- Made of crystallized seafoam.
    
- Moves like flowing water instead of feathers.
    
- Leaves a trailing spiral of mist when drawn.
    

---

## 🌊 Abilities

### 1️⃣ Megatide Impact

Upon impact:

- Summons a collapsing sphere of oceanic pressure.
    
- Creates a localized tsunami burst.
    
- Generates crushing deep-sea pressure equivalent to abyssal trenches.
    

---

### 2️⃣ Leviathan Surge

When fired at full power:

- A spectral sea serpent forms around the arrow mid-flight.
    
- The serpent roars with thunderous ocean sound.
    
- Impact zone floods instantly.
    

---

### 3️⃣ Domain of the Sea King

If shot into the sky:

- Clouds blacken.
    
- Lightning strikes.
    
- Torrential rain begins.
    
- The battlefield becomes an oceanic arena.
    

---

### 4️⃣ Abyssal Binding

If it misses:

- It transforms into water.
    
- Reforms behind the target.
    
- Strikes again from an unpredictable angle.
    

---

## 🌌 Ultimate Mode – “Oceanic Cataclysm”

The arrow splits into:

- 100 high-pressure water lances.
    
- Each guided by tidal currents.
    
- All converging simultaneously.
    

After impact:

- The ground becomes a temporary sea.
    
- Massive whirlpool forms.
    
- Silence follows.
    

---

## ⚡ Power Level

- Cannot be evaporated by normal fire.
    
- Absorbs water sources to amplify damage.
    
- Stronger near oceans, rivers, storms.
    

---

## 🔱 Symbolism

This is not just a projectile.  
It represents:

- Authority over water
    
- Dominance of tides
    
- Judgment of the sea
