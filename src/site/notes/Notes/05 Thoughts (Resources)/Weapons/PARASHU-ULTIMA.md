# AXE OF PARSHURAMA — “PARASHU-ULTIMA”

## Core Nature
A divine battle-axe forged at the convergence of:
- Cosmic Justice (Dharma)
- Absolute Destruction (Kshatra)
- Infinite Energy (Brahmanic Source)

It is not merely a weapon — it is a **judgment system** that executes verdicts instantly.

---

## Structure

### 1. Blade: “Kaal-Khand”
- Made of condensed time-energy
- Cuts not just matter, but **existence itself**
- Cannot be blocked, reflected, or evaded
- Any entity struck is removed from:
  - Physical reality  
  - Memory of all beings  
  - Causal timeline  

---

### 2. Handle: “Rudra Danda”
- Bound to the user’s life force
- Converts every injury into power
- Grants:
  - Infinite strength scaling
  - Regeneration faster than destruction
  - Immunity to aging, disease, and death

---

### 3. Core Gem: “Amrita Bind”
- Eternal essence embedded at the center
- Grants:
  - Immortality to the wielder
  - Infinite stamina
  - Resistance to all known and unknown forces

---

### 4. Runic Inscription: “Dharma Code”
- Evaluates the moral alignment of targets
- Only allows strikes when:
  - Target violates cosmic law
  - Target threatens balance of existence
- Overrides user hesitation and doubt

---

## Abilities

### 1. Absolute Severance
Cuts through:
- Gods
- Demons
- Reality layers
- Concepts (time, space, fate)

---

### 2. Immortal Ascension
User becomes:
- Immortal
- Indestructible
- Beyond aging or decay
- Exists outside normal causality

---

### 3. Ultra Strength Amplification
- Strength scales exponentially with each swing
- No upper limit
- Strength is dynamically recalculated based on threat level

---

Summons:

  - Combat mastery

---

## Passive Effects

- Passive aura of fear to enemies
- Allies gain courage and strength
- Lies and deception are automatically nullified within range
- Any injustice within proximity destabilizes reality until corrected

---

## Weaknesses

- Cannot be wielded by those with corrupt intent
- Requires alignment with Dharma; otherwise:
  - Becomes unbearably heavy
  - Rejects the user
- If misused:
  - May judge the wielder itself
  - Can strip immortality as punishment

---

## Final State: “Mahakaal Mode”
When fully awakened:
- The axe and user become one entity
- All attacks become unavoidable
- The user transcends:
  - Space
  - Time
  - Causality

At this point, the axe is no longer a weapon —  
it becomes **existence itself deciding what should remain**.
