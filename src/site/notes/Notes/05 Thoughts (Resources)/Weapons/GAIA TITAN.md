# 🌍 **GAIA TITAN – The Mega Earth Arrow**

---

## 🔱 Core Identity

**Name Engraved on Shaft:**

> “EARTH” — carved in deep continental script, filled with molten emerald crystal.

The word **EARTH** isn’t just written — it’s embedded using compressed tectonic stone and glowing blue magma veins that pulse like the planet’s core.

---

## 🪨 Physical Structure

### 🟫 1. Arrowhead – _Tectonic Core Blade_

- Forged from **condensed mantle stone**.
    
- Surface looks like cracked continental plates.
    
- Lava glows faintly through fissures.
    
- Edges are razor-sharp basalt crystal.
    
- Weight: Extremely heavy, but magically balanced.
    

When it strikes:

- Causes localized seismic shockwaves.
    
- Splits ground in a controlled tectonic rupture.
    
- Creates temporary stone pillars on impact.
    

---

### 🌳 2. Shaft – _World Spine Column_

- Crafted from petrified World Tree wood.
    
- Grain patterns resemble mountain ranges.
    
- The name **EARTH** runs vertically along it.
    
- Moss and tiny glowing fungi grow naturally along the shaft.
    

The shaft regenerates itself using absorbed soil and minerals.

---

### 🍃 3. Fletching – _Elemental Biome Wings_

Each feather represents a biome:

- 🌲 Forest Feather (green)
    
- 🏜 Desert Feather (gold)
    
- ❄ Tundra Feather (icy blue)
    
- 🌊 Ocean Feather (deep teal)
    

When fired, the fletching shifts depending on terrain.

---

## 🌋 Abilities

### 1. **Tectonic Impact**

On impact:

- Creates a circular earthquake shockwave.
    
- Raises a ring of stone walls around the strike zone.
    

### 2. **Gaia Shield**

If planted in the ground:

- Generates a dome of stone, vines, and earth.
    
- Absorbs incoming attacks.
    
- Converts damage into healing energy for allies standing on soil.
    

### 3. **Gravity Pulse**

Mid-flight activation:

- Increases gravity around the arrow.
    
- Pulls enemies toward the landing zone.
    

### 4. **Continental Recall**

The arrow can return by tunneling underground.  
You feel the earth rumble before it resurfaces in your hand.

---

## 🌎 Ultimate Ability — “Planetfall Judgment”

When fully charged:

- The arrow glows like a molten planet core.
    
- The sky darkens.
    
- A phantom image of Earth appears above the battlefield.
    
- A massive stone titan fist slams down at the target point.
    

This attack reshapes terrain permanently.

---

## 🧠 Lore Concept

This arrow was not forged — it was **awakened**.

It contains:

- The memory of mountains.
    
- The patience of stone.
    
- The fury of volcanoes.
    
- The stability of gravity.
    

Only someone with unbreakable will can lift it.  
Anyone unstable gets crushed by its weight instantly.
