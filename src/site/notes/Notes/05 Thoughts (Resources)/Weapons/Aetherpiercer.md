# 🏹 **Aetherpiercer – The Arcane Phase Arrow**

## 🔮 Classification

- **Type:** Enchanted Ammunition
    
- **Rarity:** Legendary
    
- **Attunement:** Not required (but performs best when used by a spellcaster or arcane archer)
    

---

## 🧪 Core Concept

This arrow is built around **phase manipulation + spell-channeling**.  
It does not simply deal extra damage — it **ignores conventional defenses**.

---

## ⚙️ Physical Description

- Shaft: White ash wood veined with faint blue luminescent lines.
    
- Arrowhead: Crystalline metal (looks like frozen lightning).
    
- Fletching: Translucent feathers that flicker like heat distortion.
    
- When drawn, it hums at a high frequency — like a tuning fork.
    

---

## ✨ Primary Enchantment: _Phase Breach_

When fired:

- The arrow partially shifts into the Ethereal Plane.
    
- It ignores:
    
    - Non-magical armor
        
    - Physical cover (except total magical barriers)
        
    - Damage resistance to piercing (non-magical)
        

Mechanically (D&D-style):

- The attack is made with advantage against armored targets.
    
- Ignores AC granted by armor (uses Dex + magical bonuses only).
    

---

## 🔥 Secondary Enchantment: _Spell Conduit_

Before firing, the user may infuse a spell of 3rd level or lower into the arrow.

When it hits:

- The spell is cast on the target automatically.
    
- No additional action required.
    
- Spell save DC = user’s spell DC.
    

Example uses:

- Infuse **Fireball** → Impact detonation.
    
- Infuse **Hold Person** → Instant paralysis.
    
- Infuse **Counterspell** → Interrupt spell mid-cast if timed correctly.
    

---

## 💥 Ultimate Effect: _Aether Rupture_ (1 use only)

On a critical hit:

- The arrow tears open a micro-rift.
    
- Target must make a Con save.
    
- On failure:
    
    - Takes 8d10 force damage.
        
    - Is partially displaced (stunned 1 round).
        
- On success:
    
    - Half damage, no stun.
        

After this triggers, the arrow disintegrates into arcane dust.

---

## ⚖️ Balance Considerations

This is powerful. To prevent abuse:

- Limited supply (crafted individually).
    
- Requires rare material: "Starfall Crystal."
    
- Infusion spell slot is consumed.
    
- If you miss, the arrow vanishes into the Ethereal Plane permanently.
    

---

## 🧠 Tactical Use Cases

- Assassinating a heavily armored warlord.
    
- Sniping a wizard hiding behind Shield.
    
- Disabling a dragon mid-flight.
    
- Opening combat with battlefield control.
