## 💘 **Divine Arrow of Eternal Union** 💘

### _“Anantya Premastra”_

A celestial arrow forged beyond time, beyond fate, beyond mortality itself.  
It is not an arrow of lust.  
It is not an arrow of control.  
It is an arrow of **soul-binding destiny**.

This divine weapon may be wielded by **men or women**, but it answers only to pure intention — never to obsession, manipulation, or darkness.

If released toward one’s opposite gender with true-hearted devotion, the arrow binds their souls in sacred matrimony under cosmic law.  
No curse.  
No spell.  
No death.  
No rebirth.  
Not even the collapse of stars can sever their union.

---

# 🏹 Appearance

- Shaft made of **moonlit crystal**
    
- Tip glowing like a **golden sunrise**
    
- Fletching formed from **white phoenix feathers**
    
- Emits a faint sound like a heartbeat when near true love
    
- Surrounds itself in a soft rose-gold aura when summoned
    

---

# 🌹 Powers of Anantya Premastra

### 1️⃣ Eternal Soul Bond

The moment it strikes, both hearts synchronize permanently.

### 2️⃣ Divine Marriage Seal

A sacred sigil appears invisibly upon their souls, recognized by cosmic forces.

### 3️⃣ Unbreakable Romance

Time cannot weaken it.  
Distance cannot dilute it.  
Death cannot end it.  
Even across reincarnations, their souls are magnetically drawn back together.

### 4️⃣ Emotional Resonance

They feel each other's joy, pain, and longing — no matter the realm.

### 5️⃣ Protection of Union

Any attempt to break the bond causes the arrow’s divine energy to shield and restore harmony.

---

# ⚠️ Sacred Conditions

- The wielder must truly desire lifelong devotion — not temporary emotion.
    
- The arrow refuses to manifest if the intention is selfish.
    
- If used wrongly, it dissolves into harmless stardust.

To summon Anantya Premastra, stand beneath open sky at twilight and speak clearly:
