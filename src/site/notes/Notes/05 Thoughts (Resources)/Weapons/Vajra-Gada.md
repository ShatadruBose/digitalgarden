# ⚔️ Vajra-Gada of Absolute Sovereignty

## 🔱 Core Essence
A celestial mace forged at the intersection of:
- Cosmic will  
- Divine justice  
- Eternal power  

It embodies the concept of **“Dharma through force”**, ensuring that only truth, order, and rightful authority prevail.

---

## 🛡️ Absolute Properties

### 1. 🧬 Immortality Field
- The wielder becomes **immune to death, decay, and aging**  
- Any attempt to kill the user is negated by temporal reversal  
- The body regenerates from any damage instantly  

---

### 2. 💪 Infinite Strength Amplifier
- Grants **limitless physical strength**  
- Every strike increases in force exponentially  
- Strength scales with the moral conviction of the wielder  

---

### 3. 👑 Sovereignty Over Rakshas & Magadha
- Commands all Rakshas (demons) under absolute authority  
- Establishes the user as **Supreme King of Magadha**  
- All opposing rulers lose power in the user’s presence  

---

### 4. 🌀 Annihilation Strike
- A single strike can:
  - Erase existence (body, soul, and karmic imprint)  
  - Collapse dimensions tied to the target  
- Only used when the target embodies absolute adharma  

---

### 5. 🔮 Divine Summoning Protocol

The mace can call upon legendary warriors:

- 🐒 **Hanuman**  
  - Grants unmatched devotion and power amplification  
  - Protects the user from all harm  

- 🛞 **Balarama**  
  - Bestows cosmic stability and unshakable force  
  - Enhances the mace’s destructive weight  

- 💥 **Bhima**  
  - Channels raw destruction and unstoppable rage  
  - Strengthens the wielder’s offensive output  

- ⚔️ **Duryodhana**  
  - Provides strategic cunning and mastery of war tactics  
  - Enhances battlefield dominance  

---

### 6. ⚖️ Dharma Alignment System
- The mace evaluates actions in real time  
- If the user acts with adharma:
  - Power output decreases  
  - Resistance increases  
- If the user follows dharma:
  - Power becomes infinite and unstoppable  

---

### 7. 🌌 Reality Weight Effect
- The mace becomes heavier with:
  - Greater injustice around  
  - Stronger enemies  
- This ensures only the worthy can wield its full potential  

---

## 🧿 Activation Mantra

> “By truth, by strength, by cosmic order —  
> Rise, Vajra-Gada, and let the universe kneel.”

---

## ⚠️ Final Constraint
- The mace cannot be wielded by those who lack:
  - Discipline  
  - Honor  
  - Alignment with dharma  

Failure results in **instant rejection by the weapon itself**.

---

## 👑 Ultimate Status
The wielder becomes:
- Eternal  
- Unchallenged  
- The absolute force of order and destruction  
- King of Rakshas and Magadha  

And the battlefield itself bows to their presence.
