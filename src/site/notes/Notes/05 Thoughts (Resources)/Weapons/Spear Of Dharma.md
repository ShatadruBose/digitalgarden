# 🗡️ Spear of Dharma (धर्मास्त्र)

## 🪬 Overview
The **Spear of Dharma** is a divine, sentient weapon forged from the essence of cosmic truth (**Satya**) and righteousness (**Dharma**). It does not merely act—it judges, reveals, and enforces the moral fabric of the universe.

---

## ⚙️ Core Attributes

- **Name**: Dharma
- **Type**: Divine Spear (Sentient Astra)
- **Element**: Truth + Cosmic Law
- **Alignment**: Pure Dharma (Rejects Adharma absolutely)
- **Indestructibility**: Cannot be broken, bent, or corrupted

---

## 🔥 Abilities

### 🧠 Truth Revelation
- Instantly reveals **absolute truth** in any situation
- Can detect lies, illusions, hidden motives, and deception
- Forces beings within a radius to confront their true selves

---

### 📜 Knowledge Manifestation
- Grants the wielder access to **infinite wisdom**
- Answers any question related to:
  - Dharma (righteousness)
  - Karma (actions and consequences)
  - Time (past, present, future probabilities)
- Cannot be misused for adharmic intentions

---

### ⚡ Adharma Execution
- Automatically identifies **adharmic entities**
- Instantly destroys:
  - Evil beings
  - Corrupt souls
  - Those acting against cosmic balance
- Ineffective against:
  - Pure-hearted beings
  - Neutral or dharmic entities

---

### 👑 Yudhishthira Invocation
- Can summon **Yudhishthira**, embodiment of Dharma, as a spiritual projection
- Yudhishthira provides:
  - Strategic guidance
  - Moral judgment
  - Decision-making clarity
- Summon Duration: Limited by wielder’s purity and intent

---

### 🛡️ Dharma Shield
- Creates an **invisible kavach (shield)** when danger is detected
- Protects only if the wielder's intentions are righteous

---

## 🧬 Sentience System

- Spear chooses its wielder
- Rejects:
  - Greed
  - Ego
  - Deception
- Bonds with:
  - Honesty
  - Justice
  - Selflessness

---

## 🚫 Limitations

- Cannot be used for personal revenge
- Loses power if wielder deviates from Dharma
- Will abandon or vanish if misused

---

## 🌌 Ultimate State: "Satya Prakash"

When fully awakened:
- Emits blinding light of pure truth
- Removes all illusion from the battlefield
- Forces all beings to act according to their true nature
- Ends conflicts by exposing reality itself

---

## 🏁 Verdict
The **Spear of Dharma** is not just a weapon—it is a **judge, teacher, and executioner of cosmic law**. Only the truly righteous can wield it, and even they must remain vigilant… because Dharma watches all.
