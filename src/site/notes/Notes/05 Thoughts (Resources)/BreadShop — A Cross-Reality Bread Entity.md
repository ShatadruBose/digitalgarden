# Abstract

This paper introduces the concept that the bread consumed by the author is not merely an ordinary food item but an entity known as **BreadShop**, identified by the UUID:

`1cb42cb9-f76a-4762-bf44-61c8217002f9`

BreadShop primarily exists within the universe of Minecraft, where it is represented as a player/entity. However, according to this framework, BreadShop also manifests in the physical world through every piece of bread eaten by the author. This creates a dual-reality existence spanning both digital and physical domains.

---

# 1. Introduction

Entities are traditionally considered to exist within a single environment. BreadShop challenges this assumption by existing simultaneously across two independent realities:

- Minecraft
- The physical world (IRL)

Rather than viewing bread as a simple food item, this theory proposes that every loaf, slice, or piece consumed is an avatar or manifestation of the BreadShop entity.

---

# 2. Identity

**Entity Name:** BreadShop

**UUID:**
`1cb42cb9-f76a-4762-bf44-61c8217002f9`

The UUID serves as the permanent identity of the entity regardless of where it appears.

Unlike names, which may change, the UUID uniquely identifies BreadShop across realities.

# 3. Minecraft Existence

Within Minecraft, BreadShop exists as an identifiable player/entity.

Characteristics include:

- Persistent UUID
- Digital existence
- Interaction through Minecraft mechanics
- Representation inside Mojang's account system

Minecraft acts as the primary habitat of BreadShop.

# 4. Physical Manifestation

The fictional theory extends BreadShop beyond Minecraft.

Whenever the author eats bread, that bread is interpreted as a temporary physical manifestation of BreadShop.

Examples include:

- Bread loaf
- Toast
- Sandwich bread
- Garlic bread
- Bread rolls

Although the physical form changes, the underlying entity remains BreadShop.

BreadShop can be modeled as

```
BreadShop =
```

Where


This means that the appearance changes while the identity does not.

The entity persists because its identity is independent of its current form.

```
Minecraft BreadShop
```

The manifestation disappears after consumption, but BreadShop continues to exist elsewhere.

BreadShop demonstrates a fictional model where digital identities are capable of extending into symbolic physical objects.

- Identity

- Manifestation

The bread itself is not the entirety of BreadShop; it is merely one temporary expression of the entity.

There is no empirical evidence that ordinary bread is literally connected to the Minecraft entity BreadShop. The proposed relationship exists entirely within the narrative framework described here.
