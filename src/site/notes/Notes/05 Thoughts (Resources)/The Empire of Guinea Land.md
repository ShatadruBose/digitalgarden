# The Empire of Guinea Land (Fictional World)

> **Note:** This is a **fictional worldbuilding setting** inspired by real places and historical names. The political empire, common currency, and unified government described below are entirely fictional.

---

# The Empire of Guinea Land

## Overview

The **Empire of Guinea Land** is a fictional transcontinental empire that unites every modern nation whose name contains **Guinea** or **Guiana** under one sovereign state.

Founded under the **Imperial Charter of Guinea**, the empire stretches across **Africa**, **South America**, and **Oceania**, making it one of the largest and most culturally diverse empires in the world.

Its official currency is the **Guinea (GNS)**, inspired by the historical British guinea while serving as the common currency of the empire.

# Imperial Vision

The Empire of Guinea Land strives to unite the seven Guinea and Guiana lands into one peaceful federation, celebrating their shared names while respecting their unique histories and cultures. Under a single government and a common **Guinea (GNS)** currency, the empire promotes free movement, scientific progress, sustainable development, cultural exchange, and prosperity across three continents.
