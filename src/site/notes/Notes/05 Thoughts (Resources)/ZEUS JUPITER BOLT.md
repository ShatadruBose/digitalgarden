- **Weapon Type:** Primordial Celestial Arrow
    
- **Elemental Core:** Hypercompressed Divine Plasma

## 🏹 Physical Description

The shaft is forged from **Aetherium**, a metal that exists between lightning strikes.  
It glows electric-blue with veins of molten gold running through it.

Along the shaft, engraved in ancient celestial script:

> **ΖΕΥΣ • JUPITER • BOLT**

The lettering constantly crackles with miniature storms.

The arrowhead is shaped like a triple-pronged thunderbolt — sharp enough to pierce mountains, realms, and dimensional barriers.

The fletching is not feathers —  
It is **living storm-wind**, spiraling behind the arrow even while stationary.
