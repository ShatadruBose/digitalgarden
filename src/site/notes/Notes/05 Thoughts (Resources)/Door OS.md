# Door OS: A Concept Paper on the Operating System of the Door

## Abstract

Door OS is a conceptual operating system designed around one of the most universal objects in human life: the door. Rather than being a passive object that only opens and closes, a door becomes an intelligent computing platform capable of security, identity verification, automation, communication, and environmental awareness.

Door OS combines hardware, firmware, networking, artificial intelligence, and access control into a single software platform. The goal is to transform every door into a secure, context-aware gateway between spaces.

---

# 1. Introduction

Doors have existed for thousands of years. They protect homes, define private spaces, control movement, and provide security.

Modern technology has already added electronic locks, biometric scanners, cameras, motion sensors, and smart home integration. However, these systems are often disconnected.

Door OS proposes a unified operating system that manages every function of an intelligent door.

Instead of many independent devices, everything works under one platform.

---

# 2. Vision

The vision of Door OS is simple:

> "Every door becomes an intelligent gateway."

Instead of merely opening or closing, the door understands:

- Who is approaching
- Why they are approaching
- Whether they should be allowed
- When they should enter
- What actions should happen afterward

A door becomes a decision-making system instead of only a mechanical object.
