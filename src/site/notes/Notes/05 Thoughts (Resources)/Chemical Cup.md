# Chemical Cup

> A football tournament based on the Periodic Table of Elements.

---

# 1. Overview

The **Chemical Cup** is the official football championship of the Periodic Table.

The tournament consists of:

- **18 Main Groups** (88 teams total)
- **Lanthanum Series** (15 teams)
- **Actinium Series** (15 teams)

The Lanthanum and Actinium Series are completely separate from the main tournament and have their own competitions.

# 3. Group Stage

Every team plays every other team in its group exactly once.

### Points

- Win = 3
- Draw = 1
- Loss = 0

### Ranking

1. Points
2. Goal Difference
3. Goals Scored
4. Head-to-Head
5. Fair Play
6. Drawing of Lots

## Direct Qualification

The following teams qualify directly for the Round of 32.

- Winners of all 18 groups
- Runner-up of Group I
- Runner-up of Group XVIII
