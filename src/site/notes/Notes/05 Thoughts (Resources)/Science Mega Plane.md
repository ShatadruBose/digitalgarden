# A-10X Science Mega Plane

## Overview
A gigantic ultra-advanced aircraft designed for extreme speed, deep science missions, and full custom control.  
It is **10x faster than the Concade**, and with the **overclock mode**, it can reach up to **50x speed**.

---

## Core Features
- **Speed**: 10x base Concade speed  
- **Overclock Mode**: Up to 50x speed  
- **Size**: 10x larger than Concade  
- **Customization**: Fully modular—body, systems, interiors, and mission setup can be changed  
- **Mission Type**: Scientific exploration, high-speed transport, research operations  

---

## Scientific Systems
- Mega laboratory with multi-discipline research stations  
- Real-time data analysis core  
- Experiment modules:
  - Physics (high-energy, motion, aerodynamics)  
  - Chemistry (reaction chambers, material synthesis)  
  - Biology (bio-samples, micro labs)  
  - Astronomy (deep space observation systems)  
- Sample storage for:
  - Atmospheric gases  
  - Rock/soil samples  
  - Liquids  
  - Biological materials  
- Environmental sensors:
  - Temperature  
  - Radiation  
  - Pressure  
  - Gravity  

---

## Communication Network
- **4 Mega Antennas** with ultra-long-range capability  
- Connects with:
  - Ground stations  
  - Other aircraft  
  - Satellites  
  - Space systems  
  - Remote labs  
- Supports:
  - High-bandwidth data transfer  
  - Encrypted scientific communication  
  - Multi-node synchronization

- Autonomous navigation + propulsion
