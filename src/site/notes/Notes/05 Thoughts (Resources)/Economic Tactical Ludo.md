# 🎲 **Ludo by Shatadru Bose**

_A Strategic Economic War Variant of_ Pachisi

---

# 1️⃣ Game Overview

This is a high-complexity strategy variant featuring:

- 6 Pieces per player (1 Leader + 5 Pawns)
    
- Gold economy
    
- Terrain system (12 special squares)
    
- Leader aura system
    
- Alliances (Allowance Agreements)
    
- Advanced movement mechanics
    

Victory Condition:

- Get all 6 pieces home  
    OR
    
- If alliance mode active: last surviving non-eliminated faction wins.
    

---

# 2️⃣ Components

Per Player:

- 1 Leader (counts as 2 Pawns in combat)
    
- 5 Pawns
    
- Gold tracker
    

Board:

- Standard Pachisi/Ludo layout
    
- 12 Special Tiles (2 of each type):
    
    - Treasure
        
    - Dice
        
    - Water
        
    - Storm
        
    - Castle
        
    - Void
        

---

# 3️⃣ Core Movement Rules

### 🎯 Split Movement

Roll result may be divided among pieces.  
All movement must use full roll.

Example:  
Roll 15 → 8 forward for A, 7 backward for B (if reverse purchased).

---

### 🔁 Reverse Rule (5 Gold)

You may reverse part of a move.

Example:  
Roll 15:

- A moves 8 forward
    
- B moves 7 backward
    

Must declare before moving.

---

### 🧩 Combo Stack Rule

Stacked pieces may divide movement equally if divisible.

Example:  
2 pieces + roll 14 → each moves 7

Max stack size: unlimited (unless blocked by Storm).

Stacks cannot enter home stretch.

---

# 4️⃣ Leader System

Leader = 2 Pawns in strength.

To capture a Leader:

- 1 Leader  
    OR
    
- 2 Pawns stacked
    

If Leader captured:

- Opponent gains 4 Gold
    

---

# 👑 Leader Aura

If Leader stands on:

|Tile|Effect|
|---|---|
|Treasure|Gains +3 Gold instead of +2|
|Void|Immune to -2 Gold penalty|
|Castle|Becomes a Safe Square|
|Storm|Must pay 1 Gold or skip next move|

Leader projects dominance.  
Protect him wisely.

---

# 5️⃣ Gold Economy

## 💰 Earning Gold

|Action|Gold|
|---|---|
|Roll 10+|+1|
|Capture Pawn|+2|
|Complete Lap|+3|
|Capture Leader|+4|
|Treasure Square|+2 (once per piece per square)|

---

## 💸 Spending Gold

|Ability|Cost|
|---|---|
|Reroll|1|
|Extra Move|2|
|Freeze Opponent Pawn (1 turn)|3|
|Force Opponent Reroll|4|
|Reverse Movement|5|
|Skip Opponent Turn|10|

⚠ Skip Turn can only be used once every 5 rounds per player.

Gold Cap: 15 Maximum.

---

# 6️⃣ Special Squares

Each appears twice on board.

### 🪙 Treasure

+2 Gold (once per piece per square)

### 🎲 Dice

Extra move (cannot chain infinitely)

### 💧 Water

Move +1 extra

### 🌪 Storm

Stacks cannot enter.  
Individuals must skip it.  
Leader may enter (pays 1 Gold).

### 🏰 Castle

Only Leader may enter.  
Pawns must skip.

### 🕳 Void

If captured here:

- Victim loses 2 Gold  
    (Leader immune)
    

---

# 7️⃣ Alliances (Allowance Agreements)

Players may:

- Form temporary alliances
    
- Agree not to capture each other
    
- Share tactical zones
    

Rules:

- Alliances must be declared publicly
    
- Can be broken anytime
    
- Betrayal allowed
    

No gold trading allowed (optional rule if you want chaos mode).

This introduces political meta-game.

---

# 8️⃣ Capture Rules

- Normal capture → piece returns to base
    
- Stack captured → all return
    
- Leader captured → treated as 2 pawns lost
    

---

# 9️⃣ Endgame Rules

- Exact count required to enter home
    
- Overshoot = move invalid
    
- Stacks automatically break in final stretch
    

---

# 🔟 Turn Structure

1. Roll
    
2. Declare gold usage (if any)
    
3. Move pieces
    
4. Resolve tile effects
    
5. Resolve captures
    
6. End turn
    

---

# 🧠 Strategy Tutorial

## Early Game

- Prioritize lap completion for gold engine.
    
- Do not overspend on freeze early.
    
- Protect Leader until mid-game.
    

## Mid Game

- Control Treasure zones.
    
- Use reverse movement to escape traps.
    
- Form temporary alliances if behind.
    

## Late Game

- Save 10 Gold for critical Skip Turn.
    
- Leader should control Castle zone.
    
- Use freeze tactically to block home entry.
