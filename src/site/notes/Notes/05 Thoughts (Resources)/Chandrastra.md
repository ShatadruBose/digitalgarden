# 🌙 **Chandrastra — The Moon Summoning Arrow**

## Cosmic Classification

- **Name:** Chandrastra (The Lunar Judgment Arrow)
    
- **Type:** Divine Astral Arrow
    
- **Element:** Lunar / Astral / Time
    
- **Tier:** Celestial Artifact
    
- **Origin:** Forged from fragments of the first moonlight that touched Earth.
    

---

# 🌕 Core Concept

**Chandrastra** is an ultra-powerful arrow that channels the primordial energy of the Moon.  
When fired and **strikes the ground**, it does not merely explode — it **opens a lunar gateway** and **summons the Moon God himself** to the battlefield.

This arrow is said to hold the **authority of the night sky**.

---

# 🌙 Appearance

The arrow looks otherworldly:

- **Arrowhead:** Made of crystallized moonstone glowing silver-blue.
    
- **Shaft:** White astral metal engraved with lunar phases.
    
- **Fletching:** Feathers formed from pure starlight.
    
- **Aura:** Emits cold silver mist like moonlight over water.
    

When drawn on a bow, the arrow begins to **orbit small glowing moons around itself**.

---

# 🌌 Activation

The arrow activates when:

1. It is fired under the **night sky**.
    
2. The arrow **hits the ground**.
    
3. The lunar seal engraved on the shaft **breaks open**.
    

At that moment:

- The ground glows with a **massive lunar sigil**.
    
- The sky turns dark blue.
    
- A **gigantic full moon illusion appears overhead**.
    

---

# 🌑 The Impact Event

When Chandrastra strikes:

1. The arrow embeds into the earth.
    
2. The ground fractures in a **perfect circular crater**.
    
3. Silver light erupts upward like a pillar.
    
4. A **lunar eclipse forms in the sky**.
    

Then the summoning begins.

---

# 🌕 Summoning of the Moon God

From the pillar of moonlight emerges the **Moon God**.

Possible mythic interpretations include:

- Chandra
    
- Selene
    
- Tsukuyomi
    

Depending on the world or mythology, the arrow may summon **one of these lunar rulers**.

---

# 🌙 Powers of the Moon God Upon Summoning

When the Moon God arrives, the battlefield becomes **Lunar Dominion**.

### 1. **Tidal Authority**

The Moon God can control tides and gravity.

Effects:

- Enemies move in **slow lunar gravity**.
    
- Allies move with **grace and speed**.
    

---

### 2. **Silver Moon Beams**

Columns of moonlight fall from the sky.

These beams:

- Freeze enemies in lunar ice
    
- Heal allies with calm lunar energy
    

---

### 3. **Night Dominion**

The battlefield becomes eternal night.

Stars appear even during daytime.

---

### 4. **Lunar Reflection Shield**

All attacks reflect like light off a mirror.

---

### 5. **Moonfall Judgment**

The Moon God summons a **falling fragment of the Moon**.

Impact power:

- City-level destruction
    
- Leaves a glowing crater called **The Lunar Scar**
    

---

# 🌘 Passive Powers of the Arrow

Even before firing, Chandrastra grants the wielder:

- Night vision
    
- Lunar regeneration
    
- Astral awareness
    
- Protection from dark magic
    

---

# 🌕 Ultimate Event — **Full Moon Cataclysm**

If Chandrastra is fired during a **full moon**, the summoning becomes far stronger.

Instead of a temporary appearance:

The Moon God manifests in **true celestial form**.

Effects include:

- Massive tidal waves
    
- Lunar meteors
    
- Time slowing across the battlefield
    
- The sky becoming a giant glowing moon.
    

---

# 🌙 Weakness

The arrow cannot be used:

- During a solar eclipse
    
- Under artificial light without the night sky
    
- By someone who disrespects lunar power.
    

---

# 🌌 Legendary Lore

Ancient texts say:

> "When the Moon touches the Earth through Chandrastra,  
> the boundary between sky and world dissolves."

The arrow is rumored to be hidden in a **lunar temple that appears only once every thousand nights**.
